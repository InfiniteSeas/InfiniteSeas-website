Infinite Seas Studios

Infinite Seas Studios is crafting a fully on-chain maritime trading and diplomatic social game on Move L2. Here, victors claim the stakes of the vanquished as the game rolls on. Our aim is to experiment with the extent to which a game can foster microtransactions and player-to-player trading, all powered by our AMM infrastructure and backend engine.

As a team of dedicated MMO enthusiasts, we draw inspiration from classics like Heroes of Might and Magic, Mir2, RuneScape, Bat Mud, and Uncharted Waters IV. We're committed to creating a world not just to play in, but to live in.

Led by the best innovative creators and seasoned devs, we're here to ignite excitement. Game on, and stay tuned.
